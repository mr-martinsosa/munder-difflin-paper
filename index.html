About Us:

Our company was established in 1982 with the vision of not only having
the best quality of paper, but paper of varying types; types that have a different sense of feel or material.

Our Paper

Try our simple bamboo paper, starting at the low price of $14.99/lb!

Our classic and fan favorite. Keep it simple at $6.99/lb.

This is the CEO

His name is Bob Green

He enjoys long walks on the beach, team outings and taking care of his bamboo trees.

Bob believes in the future of paper, in an era of digital technology. He understands the value of people who believe in using paper for reading and writing; not locking their eyes to a screen.


This is the Co-Founder

Her name is Stephanie Seasons

She enjoys watering her flowers, playing with her pets and writing about nature.

Stephanie and Bob started this company because they shared a passion for paper, a passion many people do not have. They understand the neccessity of paper in day to day life.
